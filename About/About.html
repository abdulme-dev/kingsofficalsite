Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

Key milestones in our school's distinguished history

1999

Foundation

King's Academy was founded by Engineer Samuel Zewdu with a vision to provide national-class international education in our community.

2005

First Graduating Class

Our inaugural class of 32 students graduated, with 100% university acceptance rate including prestigious international institutions.

2010

New Campus

We moved to our current state-of-the-art campus featuring modern classrooms, science labs, and athletic facilities.

2015

IB Authorization

Became an authorized International Baccalaureate World School, offering the prestigious IB Diploma Programme.

2020

20th Anniversary

Celebrated two decades of educational excellence with over 1,200 alumni making impacts around the world.
