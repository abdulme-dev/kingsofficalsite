Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries about King's Academy

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We also offer before and after-school care programs for families who need extended hours.

What is the admissions process?

The admissions process includes submitting an application, academic records, letters of recommendation, and participating in an interview. We accept applications year-round based on availability.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer need-based financial aid to qualified families. Applications for financial aid are considered separately from admissions decisions and are based on demonstrated financial need.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports teams, music and arts programs, academic clubs, and community service opportunities. Activities vary by grade level and season.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting our admissions office at admissions@kingsacademy.com or by calling (+251) 512-3456 ext. 101. Tours are typically available Monday through Friday.
